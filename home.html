KAPSARC WebGIS

Putting your research on the Map

The WebGIS platform allows KAPSARCians (research & advisors)
to search, analyze, visualize, and disseminate spatial data
based on self-serve and on-demand manner through the web

Capabilities

Spatial Data Discovery

Search and explore spatial data hosted internally or at external locations.

Web Map Viewer

Self-serve simple web mapping environment which allows you to add layers, customize styles, and export and print out maps.

GIS Web Apps

A library of geospatial web applications developed as a result of ongoing and previous KAPSARC projects.





GIS Usecases

1. Understanding where

Spatial analysis is not just about understanding where objects are located, it is also about the spatial characteristics of these objects. You may want to know how large an object is, or you may want to describe an object in terms of its geometric properties, such as area, perimeter, length, height, and volume.

Although individual objects have their own geometric properties (size and shape), a collection of objects take on additional properties such as extent and distribution












Sample: how large is KAPSARC’s Solar farm in square meters?




Sample: What is the extent of Oil fields in Saudi Arabia??
